---
title: "8. Governance and Community"
weight: 8
description: "How the ontology is governed and how contributors can participate."
---

WattSchema is developed as an open-source project with transparent governance. Contributions are welcomed from data center operators, equipment manufacturers, software and analytics providers, standards organizations and researchers, and any other interested party. 

The ontology is available on GitHub and is released under a Berkeley Software Distribution (BSD) open-source license.
